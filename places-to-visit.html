Oras Nou Neighborhoods

Get to know the true character of Oras Nou exploring its diverse neighborhoods and districts

From historic districts rich with culture to vibrant hubs buzzing with new businesses, our neighborhoods offer plenty of ways to explore like a local. Enjoy a beach town vibe in Oceanside, dance the night away in Downtown, explore the magic of filmmaking in Highland, dine at on-trend eateries in Newtown, discover the intersection of art and history in Oldtown, and much more across town.

Explore Oras Nou Districts

Downtown

A bustling commercial and business sphere with gorgeous skyscrapers and busy avenues full of clubs and activities. The center of Oras Nou's commerce and governance and home to the largest library.

Things to Do in Downtown

Highland

Minecraftia's Entertainment Capitol. Home to some of the country's largest film and media companies as well as a number of production studios. Here you will also find many entertaining experiences such as theme parks and theaters.

Things to Do in Highland

Oceanside

Miles of gorgeous coastline and warm soft sand. Stay in the sun at an outdoor shopping center or cool off indoors at a resort hotel or movie theater.

Things to Do in Oceanside

Newtown

A modern neighborhood centered around community and wellbeing. Designed to be a dynamic city with a working relationship with nature.

Things to Do in Newtown

Oldtown

A historic town nestled in the mountains. Once home to the first civilization in the Oras Nou territory: a quaint fishing village with hillside farms.

Things to Do in Oldtown

Northwood

A wide valley of cozy living and bustling commercial avenues. Home to the Riverwood Nature Preserve, a gorgeous river passing through a natural valley from east to west.

Things to Do in Northwood

Airport City

A micro-district in Oceanside for general, commercial, and recreational aviation. Features many plane-spotting attractions, shopping malls, and other fun aviation-enthusiast destinations.

Things to Do in Airport City

Industrial City

A manufacturing and distribution hub located right off the Port of Oras Nou cargo terminal. Credited as one of the flattest land masses in the Oras Nou Territories.

Things to Do in Industrial City
