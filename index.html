Why Oras Nou?

Sand & Sun

Bask in the golden rays on our mile-long beaches, perfect for a day of relaxation or adventure. Enjoy swimming, surfing, or simply taking a leisurely stroll along the shore. Our pristine coastline is a haven for beach lovers of all ages.

Urban Excitement

Dive into the bustling energy of our commercial districts. With world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment options, there's always something happening. Explore high-end boutiques, savor gourmet cuisine, and catch the latest blockbuster at one of our state-of-the-art theaters.

Culture and Creativity

Immerse yourself in Oras Nou’s exploding art and culture scene. From contemporary galleries and live music venues to historic theaters and street art, creativity thrives here. Don’t miss our annual arts festivals, which draw talent from all over the world.

Business and Innovation

As a hub for business and innovation, Oras Nou is home to many corporate headquarters and a thriving startup scene. Our busy business centers are the heartbeat of the city, making it a prime destination for professionals and entrepreneurs alike.

Entertainment Capital

With a strong film and entertainment industry, Oras Nou rivals even the biggest entertainment hubs. Experience the glamour and excitement of movie premieres, celebrity sightings, and visit iconic filming locations around the city.
