Introduction

Oras Nou is a busy city with many districts and neighborhoods. It's important to make sure you know how to get around in order to best utilize our efficient and well-equiped public transit system.

Fairs and Travel Cards

Before you can utilize public transit you must either Purchase a Travel Card from a kiosk at train stations and transit centers or utilize the GO-O.N. mobile app. We highly recommend downloading the GO-ON mobile app to your smart phone. This app allows you to load money onto a virtual travel card and scan it easily when entering a bus or train. More information is available below.

There are multiple cards and tickets to choose from depending on your travel needs. All passes and tickets have restrictions based on type of pass purchased

  • One-Way tickets can be purchased and will provide ONE swipe or scan. One-way passes cover line transfers for up to 2 hours after first scanned.
  • All-Day Passes will allow unlimited uses for a 24 hour period after their first use
  • Monthly Passes allow 30-days of unlimted uses beggining after first use

    • Restrictions apply. See below for details.

GO->ON Mobile App

For the best public transit experience, download the GO-ON mobile app to your smartphone. This government-sponsored app allows users to create virtual Travel Cards and load funds for the easiest and most straightforward travel. In additoon to virtual cards, the app also allows users to
  • View Up-to-date Transit Schedules
  • Plan Your Trip
  • Schedule Rideshare Trips
  • Locate Transit Stops and Stations
  • Report Incidents
To download GO->ON, visit the appstore on your device and search for GO-ON. Open the app with the Blue G logo with the author Oras Nou MTS. Follow instructions in the app to create your virtual Travel Card and load funds.
