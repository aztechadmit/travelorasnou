Things to Do

Art & Culture

Explore art galleries and entire districts exploding with culture and colorful surroundings.

Beautiful Beaches

Enjoy swimming, surfing, or simply taking a leisurely stroll along the shore. Our pristine coastline is a haven for beach lovers of all ages.

Entertainment

Experience the glamour and excitement of movie premieres, celebrity sightings, and visit iconic filming locations around the city.

Live the Nightlife

When the sun goes down, Oras Nou comes alive. Experience the vibrant nightlife with a diverse array of bars, clubs, and live music venues. Dance the night away or enjoy a quiet drink at a rooftop bar with stunning city views.
Whether you're here for the sun-soaked beaches, the thriving arts and culture, or the dynamic business landscape, Oras Nou has something to offer everyone.
